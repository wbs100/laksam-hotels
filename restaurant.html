Our Culinary Story

At Jaide Regency, we blend traditional Sri Lankan flavors with contemporary culinary techniques to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Located in the cultural capital of Kandy, our restaurant offers panoramic views of the city and the surrounding hills. Our chefs source the freshest local ingredients, including spices from the nearby markets and seafood from the coastal regions.

Whether you're looking for an intimate dinner or a grand celebration, our elegant dining spaces and attentive service will make your occasion truly special.
